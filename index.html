GlitchMatic

Our exciting journey into the world of game development

About Game Development

"GlitchMatic" is a 2D pixel-art roguelike adventure set in a world corrupted by glitches...

The core gameplay revolves around fast reflexes and strategic thinking...

The game features a unique pixel art style that is heavily influenced by glitch aesthetics...

"GlitchMatic" is designed to cater to both casual players and hardcore gamers...

Our development philosophy is to blend the nostalgic charm of pixel art with modern game design principles...

Initially, "GlitchMatic" will be available on PC, but we plan to explore potential expansions to console platforms...

About Our Company

Pixels Up, founded in 2024, is a passionate indie game development studio focused on creating unique and immersive gameplay experiences. Our team specializes in pixel art, roguelike mechanics, and innovative narrative design. We aim to craft games that challenge players, pushing them to think strategically while delivering visually striking and memorable experiences. "GlitchMatic" is our latest project, and we are excited to bring this glitch-filled adventure to the world.
